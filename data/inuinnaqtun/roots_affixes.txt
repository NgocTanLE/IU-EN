## NOTE
Ref: Lowe Ronald, grammar book, 1985, page 2
[morphophonological phenomena] if a wordbase ending in -k, when adding the grammatical suffix -mun (towards), the final -l of the wordbase will change to -ng-.
Ex: umingmak (muskox) => umingmagnum pihuktuq (he walked towards the muskox)

In the case of following by another word beginning with an m-, the final -k is intact.
Ex: umingmak mamaqtuq (muskos tastes good)
